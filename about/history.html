Chronicle of Growth

Cultivating a sustainable future for the region.

From our humble beginnings as a local agricultural cooperative, we have evolved into a regional leader in food security. Our history is a journey of scaling traditional wisdom with modern industrial precision to nourish millions.

1998

Foundation in Food Supply

Established as a local agricultural cooperative focused on regional food supply, dedicated to empowering local farmers and stabilizing community nutrition.

2005

Scaling Infrastructure

Transformation through the acquisition of a major logistics hub, enabling the efficient distribution of crops from 50,000 hectares of farmland across the province.

2014

Agricultural Energy Synergy

Integrating renewable energy through our Biomass-to-Power program, specifically designed to provide sustainable energy for rural agricultural processing communities.

2022

Leading Regional Food Security

Official expansion into the South-East Asian market, establishing Agrinas as a pivotal leader in the regional framework for sustainable food security and agricultural resilience.

Continuing the Legacy

Today, PT Agrinas Pangan Nusantara stands as a beacon of progress in the agricultural sector. We continue to bridge the gap between traditional farming and modern food security needs, architecting a sustainable future for the earth and its people.
